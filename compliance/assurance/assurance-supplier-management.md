<span data-ttu-id="b4a57-115">根据 6 个不同的数据处理类别确定 2013 年 4 月 1 日要求的范围，可在 SSPA 中批准供应商作为其注册的一部分。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-115">DPR requirements are scoped based on six distinct data processing categories a supplier can be approved for as part of their enrollment in SSPA.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-116">这些类别用于标识与供应商向 Microsoft 提供的服务相关联的风险。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-116">These categories are used to identify the risk associated with the services a supplier provides to Microsoft.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-117">供应商的数据处理配置文件确定哪些函数在范围内被视为在范围内，以提供适当的数据保护。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-117">The supplier's data processing profile determines which DPR controls are considered in-scope to provide appropriate data protection.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-118">处理被视为高风险的数据的供应商必须遵守所有 2016 年 4 月 1 日要求，并且可能还需要提供独立的合规性验证。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-118">Suppliers who process data that is considered a higher risk must comply with all DPR requirements and may also need to provide independent verification of compliance.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-119">Microsoft 购买工具在允许供应商进行采购之前验证所有供应商的 SSPA 状态，包括符合适用于该 SUPPLIER 的部分。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-119">Microsoft purchasing tools validate the SSPA status of all suppliers, including compliance with applicable portions of the DPR, prior to allowing the procurement of that supplier.</span></span>

## <a name="what-types-of-subprocessors-provide-services-for-microsoft"></a><span data-ttu-id="b4a57-120">哪些类型的子处理器为 Microsoft 提供服务？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-120">What types of subprocessors provide services for Microsoft?</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="b4a57-121">"子处理者"是 Microsoft 参与的第三方，其职责包括处理 Microsoft 作为处理者所负责的 Microsoft 个人数据。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-121">A 'subprocessor' is a third party that Microsoft engages whose duties include processing Microsoft Personal Data for which Microsoft is a processor.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-122">Microsoft 的子处理者分为三个不同的类别。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-122">Microsoft's subprocessors fall into three distinct categories.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-123">每个用户都必须证明符合 SSPA，然后才能代表 Microsoft 处理客户数据。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-123">Each must demonstrate compliance with the SSPA before they can process customer data on Microsoft's behalf.</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-124">**技术** 子处理者提供用于提供特定 Microsoft 联机服务的技术。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-124">**Technology** subprocessors provide technologies used to deliver specific Microsoft online services.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-125">如果客户部署这些服务之一，为该服务标识的子处理者可以处理、存储或以其他方式访问客户数据或个人数据，同时帮助提供该服务。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-125">If a customer deploys one of these services, the subprocessors identified for that service may process, store, or otherwise access customer data or personal data while helping to provide that service.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-126">**辅助** 子处理者提供支持、操作和维护联机服务的服务。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-126">**Ancillary** subprocessors provide services that support, operate, and maintain the online services.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-127">如果客户部署其中一项服务，则标识的子处理者可能在提供辅助服务的同时处理、存储或以其他方式访问有限的客户数据或个人数据。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-127">If a customer deploys one of these services, the subprocessors identified may process, store, or otherwise access limited customer data or personal data while providing their ancillary services.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-128">**员工扩充** 子处理者有两种不同的形式：在这两种方案中，个人数据仅驻留在 Microsoft 设施、Microsoft 系统上，并受 Microsoft 策略和监管。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-128">**Staff Augmentation** subprocessors take two different forms: In both scenarios, Personal Data resides only in Microsoft facilities, on Microsoft systems, and is subject to Microsoft policies and supervision.</span></span>

    - <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-129">第一种员工扩充形式提供支持、运营和维护 Microsoft 联机服务的员工。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-129">The first form of staff augmentation provides staff that supports, operates, and maintains Microsoft online services.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-130">在履行他们的责任时，这些子处理者可能会向客户数据或个人数据公开。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-130">While fulfilling their responsibilities, these subprocessors may be exposed to customer data or personal data.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-131">例如，子处理器可能在 Microsoft 服务器上执行远程疑难解答，而这样做可能会向服务器故障转储日志中的客户数据的代码段公开。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-131">For example, a subprocessor may perform remote troubleshooting on a Microsoft server and while doing so may be exposed to snippets of customer data in a server crash dump log.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-132">第二种员工扩充形式涉及与 Microsoft 全职员工一起合作以支持、运营和维护 Microsoft 联机服务的下级处理者。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-132">The second form of staff augmentation involves subprocessors who work side by side with Microsoft full-time employees to support, operate, and maintain Microsoft online services.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-133">在 Microsoft 全职员工的工作中，这些子处理者可能会向化名数据公开。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-133">These subprocessors may be exposed to pseudonymized data as part of their work alongside Microsoft full-time employees.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="b4a57-134">技术和辅助子处理者需要实现访问控制，以遵守 Microsoft 的数据保护要求 (或) 。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-134">Technology and Ancillary subprocessors are required to implement access controls in compliance with Microsoft's Data Protection Requirements (DPR).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-135">这些要求符合或超过 Microsoft 在 OST (联机服务条款中向) 。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-135">These requirements meet or exceed the contractual commitments Microsoft makes to its customers in the Online Service Terms (OST).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b4a57-136">执行员工扩充工作的供应商受针对 Microsoft 全职员工的相同访问控制。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b4a57-136">Suppliers who perform staff augmentation work are subject to the same access controls in place for Microsoft full-time employees.</span></span>
